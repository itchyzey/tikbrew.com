About us

T I K B R E W was established in February 2024 with the goal of offering a wide selection of affordable and high-quality beverages to meet the diverse tastes and preferences of our customers. Since its inception, our brand has prided itself on delivering beverages that not only cater to coffee lovers but also offer an extensive variety of non-coffee options.

Our Menu

  • Matcha

    Matcha cold brew is a refreshing and energizing drink made by steeping high-quality matcha powder in cold water, often enhanced with milk or sweeteners.

  • Spanish Latte

    A Spanish latte is a delightful coffee drink that combines espresso, milk, and a touch of sweetness from condensed milk or sugar.

  • Creamy Choco

    The creamy allure of chocolate is an irresistible delight that captures hearts and taste buds alike. Its velvety texture glides across the palate, offering a symphony of rich, indulgent flavors that awaken the senses.

  • Strawberry Cloud

    Strawberry cloud drinks are the ultimate fusion of whimsy and refreshment, combining the sweet, fruity essence of ripe strawberries with the dreamy, airy texture of whipped cream or foamed milk.

  • Hazel Nut

    A hazelnut drink is a cozy embrace in a cup, blending the earthy, nutty richness of roasted hazelnuts with creamy textures and subtle sweetness.

  • Caramel Macchiato

    A caramel macchiato is a harmonious blend of bold espresso, velvety steamed milk, and the sweet indulgence of caramel, creating a beverage that's as rich in flavor as it is in texture.
